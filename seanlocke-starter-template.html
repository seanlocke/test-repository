Sean Locke is an interface and brand designer based in beautiful Los Angeles. I've been designing for the web since 2000.

What I believe

I believe in the economic power of clear visual design & intuitive interactions. I believe good design is good business.

Connecting the dots backwards it looks like I've been a designer since my childhood in Toronto writing computer programs and learning calligraphy.

I am inspired by Bruce Mau, Dieter Rams, & Bill Moggridge.
